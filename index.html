01 — About Me

Making sense of the noise (so you don't have to).

I'm Jagadeeshwar Korra, an Engagement Manager at Tiger Analytics. My job description is usually a mix of strategic advisor, data translator, and occasional firefighter.

Over the last decade, I've partnered with global names in retail, FMCG, and telecom to turn "big data" from a boardroom headache into something actually useful. I'm a firm believer that while algorithms are powerful, they only really matter if they solve a human problem or make someone's workday a little easier.

A few things I've picked up along the way:

  • ·It takes a village: I've had the privilege of leading teams of up to 55 brilliant analysts. My role is mostly making sure they have the right map and enough headspace to do what they do best.
  • ·The "Boring" stuff matters: I tend to focus on the unglamorous but essential foundations — like demand forecasting and inventory optimisation. It's not always flashy, but it has helped my clients realise over $80M in measurable value.
  • ·Data is a universal language: Having managed relationships across three continents, I've learned that whether you're in London or Chicago, everyone just wants a dashboard they can actually trust.

I don't believe in being the loudest voice in the room. I'd much rather be the person building the scalable platforms and planning systems that let the results speak for themselves.

When I'm not untangling complex data landscapes, you can usually find me navigating the equally complex (but much more rewarding) landscape of London's food scene.
